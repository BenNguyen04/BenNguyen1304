My projects 

Being able to develop iconography with a consistent look and feel is an important skill for most designers to have.In addition, being able to create effective marketing collateral for employers and clients is an incredibly valuable skill and something that I definitely want to be involved in at various stages of my career. 

So I took upon my biggest project so far which was to design and create the UI and home/landing page of PEACH Consulting agency in Vietnam. I was tasked to create a design in which offers the visitors, functions for customer service, hotline/contacts and everything else about the business(route to other pages). 

During the summer of 2019, I designed this in Adobe Photoshop as well as Adobe XD to make sure it looked professional, however, I was not involved in any front or back end development of the website, only the design.
