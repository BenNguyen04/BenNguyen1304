This is one of my first projects where I helped design a poster banner for a Vietnamese supermarket in Rotterdam. This banner was presented in celebration of a huge import of delicious Vietnamese food coming to The Netherlands.

The event was the introduction of a huge import of Vietnamese foods and products into sale for a supermarket. I was given the task of designing the banners for the event such as the ones you see in the photograp taken. A total of around 60 people attended the event during the afternoon which was a huge sucess. The banners designed helped attracted many passerbys(especially Dutch) to the event/sale.
