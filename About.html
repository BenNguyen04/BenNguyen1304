About Me 

My name is Nguyen Vu Tien Dung or also known as BenNguyen and I’m an aspiring Vietnamese designer. 

Throughout my life, I’ve been travelling and moving around to other countries once in every few years to explore and learn from every part of the world. I’ve lived in Singapore, and Myanmar long enough to call it my second home apart from Vietnam, and the Netherlands slowly joining that list. While traveling, living, and experiencing many cultures first hand, My infatuation design and therefore, creating and illustrating has been prominent from a young age as seen through building LEGOs. I’ve also been fascinated by all the differences between us as humans, no matter the place, everytime has something unique to themselves, to their culture. Since experiencing these cultures together with my creativity, I decided to work towards redefining design concepts to unite serveral cultures for the positive so...
 
I took upon UXD course in the Netherlands as I believe it is the first step towards learning about different aspects and cultural boundries. It is very interesting to study how people from different parts of the world think and to how we should design for them. These factors are what motivate me to strive to become a relatable, smart, and successful designer.
