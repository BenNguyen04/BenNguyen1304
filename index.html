Nguyen Vu Tien Dung

Designer’s Statement 

 Design is always around us, and learning how to communicate in a visual manner has inspired me to learn more about the subject. The words ‘create’ and ‘design’ have always sparked a sense of enthusiasm and excitement within me. From an early age I have always had a creative characteristic which I feel is my most dominant. For many years I have enjoyed designing logos, drawing and coming up with creative concepts. Since learning that design is something that I’m driven towards I have gained an interest and spectrum of knowledge of anything related to design such as the psychology and principles of designing.

Personally I am a friendly and honest individual. I believe keeping active is important, I do this with my hobbies such as sports like football and cycling. I do this often due to realising that it helps to keep stress levels down so I can work efficiently without distractions. From playing team sports throughout my life I have learnt how to be effective in a team which helped me during my school life and will in the future to fit well into team environment.

I strive to share my design style of minimalistic, intricate, and effect focused style as much as possible while also satisfying people who come across it.
